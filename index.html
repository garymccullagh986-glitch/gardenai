Lawn care programme
A complete month-by-month lawn care guide covering feeding, iron sulphate, aeration, weed control, overseeding and more. Tap any month to see what to do.
🧪 Product guide
🌿
Spring/Summer Feed (High Nitrogen)
Apply March to August. Promotes lush green growth. NPK ratios like 12-4-8. Brands: Miracle-Gro Lawn Food, Evergreen Complete, Westland Lawn Feed.
Feed
🍂
Autumn/Winter Feed (High Potassium)
Apply September to November. Hardens grass for winter, builds root strength. Brands: Evergreen Autumn, Westland Autumn Lawn Food.
Feed
⚗️
Iron Sulphate (Ferrous Sulphate)
Apply spring and autumn. Kills moss, hardens grass, deepens colour. Mix 35g per litre per sq metre. Moss blackens in 2 weeks — rake out after. Brands: Maxicrop Moss Killer, Sulphate of Iron.
Iron
🌱
Lawn Weedkiller (Selective)
Apply May to September in warm settled weather. Targets dandelions, daisies, clover, plantain. Do not mow 3 days before or after. Brands: Resolva, Verdone Extra, Weedol Lawn.
Weed
🌾
Overseeding Mix
Ryegrass for wear, fescue for ornamental. Scratch, sow at 35g per sq metre, keep moist 3 weeks. Brands: Johnsons Lawn Seed, Miracle-Gro Patch Magic.
Seed
🦠
Nematodes (Biological Pest Control)
Leatherjackets: Steinernema feltiae, Aug–Oct, soil above 10C. Chafer grubs: Heterorhabditis bacteriophora, June–July. Brands: Nemasys.
Pest
📐 Mowing guide
✂️
The one-third rule
Never remove more than one third of the grass blade in a single mow.
📏
Cutting heights by season
Spring/Autumn: 30-40mm. Summer dry spells: raise to 50mm. Winter: 40-50mm. Ornamental: 15-25mm but never in drought.
🚫
When NOT to mow
Never mow frozen grass, waterlogged lawn, or during drought stress. Avoid within 3 days of weedkiller or fertiliser.
